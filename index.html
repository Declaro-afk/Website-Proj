Adoption Process

  1. Choose Your Cat: Browse, click on a profile, and apply.
  2. Submit Application: Email havecat@gmail.com with your info.
  3. Application Review & Home Check: Takes 3–5 days, virtual/in-person visit.
  4. Meet Your Cat: Meet and bond with the cat.
  5. Final Approval & Agreement: Sign and pay any fees.
  6. Bring Your Cat Home: Schedule pickup/delivery, prepare home.
